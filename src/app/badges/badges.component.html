Badges Evolution

What can you get from Badge Evolution?

By evolving your badge, you can acquire powerful stat boosts that greatly enhance your character's performance in both PvE and PvP. Here are the possible effects:

  • - All Attack increased by: 2% / 3% / 5% / 6% / 8%
  • - All Defenses increased by: 2% / 3% / 5% / 6% / 8%
  • - All cooldowns decreased by: 4% / 6% / 8% / 10% / 12%
  • - Damage in raids increased by: 4% / 6% / 8% / 10% / 12%
  • - Attack/Defense/Property Perfections increased by: 2 / 4 / 6 / 8 / 10

Note: You can modify these stats by using the "Evolution Badge Rarify Changer" item.

Suggested Badge Options

For PvE:

It's recommended to maintain two badges for PvE—one for shadow/light elements and another for fire/water elements. The following options are suggested:

  • - C: Increased damage with X element
  • - B: During raid attacks, there is a chance to deal 60% extra damage
  • - A: Increased fairy element power
  • - S: Increased damage to the opposite element of your fairy

For PvP:

It’s recommended to have four different badges, one for each class you are likely to face. The following options are suggested:

  • - C: Increased damage with X element OR chance to reflect 4500 damage
  • - B: Increased damage with X element OR chance to reflect 4500 damage
  • - A: Chance to heal a percentage of your max HP
  • - S: Increased all perfection points
  • - C PvP: Increased damage against enemies with higher max HP
  • - B PvP: Increased damage against enemies below 40% HP
  • - A PvP: Increased damage against specific class (Archer, Mage, etc.)
  • - S PvP: Increased attack and defense in PvP scenarios

Additional Notes:

While these effects are suggested, they may vary based on your playstyle and class. Experiment with different combinations to find what works best for you.
